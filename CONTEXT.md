# Workspace Routing

## Workflows

### Write a deeply-researched article
Takes a vetted topic with angle and thesis, produces a learning map and article outline, then gives editorial feedback on the author's draft.

**Entry point:** Fill in `setup/topic-brief.md`, then run stages in order.

| Stage | Folder | Job |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `stages/01_research/` | Build a learning map for the topic |
| 2 | `stages/02_scope/` | Lock down thesis, angle, and scope boundaries from the reading |
| 3 | `stages/03_outline/` | Structure the article from the scope and reading notes |
| 4 | `stages/04_editorial/` | Give editorial notes on the author's draft |
