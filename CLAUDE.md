# Article Writing Pipeline

## What this is
A pipeline that helps the author go deep on a topic they want to understand, then write about it themselves. The LLM builds the learning map, shapes the outline, and gives editorial notes on the draft. The author does all the writing and decides what to act on. Publishing is a byproduct; understanding is the point.

## Layers
- **L0** — This file: workspace identity ("where am I?")
- **L1** — `CONTEXT.md`: workflow routing ("where do I go?")
- **L2** — `stages/0N_*/CONTEXT.md`: stage contracts ("what do I do?")
- **L3** — `_config/`: stable conventions ("what rules apply?")
- **L4** — `stages/0N_*/output/`: working artifacts ("what am I working with?")
